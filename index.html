About

A graveyard fantasy action game where allies rarely stay allies

Graveyard Battle is a co-op battle action game where you and your friends must keep the cemetery under control. Dig up graves, fight and bury monsters, and use every tool you have to survive.

But monsters are not your only threat. If even one teammate loses a fight against the undead, you may have to bury them too. Then the match changes instantly: a former ally can return as a zombie, skeleton, or vampire.

Every round leads to an unpredictable ending: one moment you save the graveyard together, and the next you are fighting for control.

Co-op that can flip

You begin as one team, but a single mistake can instantly change everyone's role in the match.

Keeper tools

Dig, fight, and bury your enemies with everything that helps you hold the line among the graves.

Unpredictable endings

Infection and ally transformations keep every match tense until the very last second.
